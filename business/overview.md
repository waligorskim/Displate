# Business Overview

*Last Updated: December 2024*
*Domain: business*

## Company Profile

- **Name**: Displate
- **HQ**: Poland (Warsaw)
- **Industry**: E-commerce, Home Decor, Art Marketplace
- **Model**: D2C (Direct to Consumer) + Artist Marketplace
- **Markets**: Global, primary focus US and EU

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Product Sales** (Primary)
   - One-time purchases of metal posters
   - Various price points by size and edition type

2. **Displate Club** (Subscription)
   - Membership program for collectors
   - Benefits: discounts, exclusive access, early releases
   - [NEEDS INFO: Subscription pricing and tiers]

3. **Artist Marketplace**
   - Revenue share with independent artists
   - Platform takes percentage of artist sales

### Customer Segments

| Segment | Description | Value |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| Collectors | Buy multiple, interested in LEs | High LTV |
| Gift Buyers | One-time or seasonal purchases | Moderate |
| Club Members | Subscription-based repeat buyers | Highest LTV |
| First-Time Buyers | New customers, acquisition focus | Growth driver |

## Market Position

### Competitors
- Funko (collectibles, different product but similar audience)
- Popmart (collectibles)
- Traditional poster companies
- Custom print services

### Unique Value Props
- No-drill magnetic mounting system
- Metal material (durability, premium feel)
- Massive catalog via artist marketplace
- Licensed IP content (Disney, Marvel, gaming)

## Key Business Challenges

Based on customer reviews and internal focus areas:

1. **International Shipping**
   - Customs fees surprise international customers
   - Returns financially prohibitive for international orders
   - 25-50% additional cost potential for international buyers

2. **Buy Box Competition** (Amazon)
   - Affects Amazon channel performance
   - Task force established to address

3. **Trend Timing**
   - Need to capitalize on cultural moments quickly
   - Competition with Funko/Popmart on trending content

## Strategic Initiatives

### AI/ML Opportunities (Identified)
- Cultural intelligence for trend prediction
- Personalized recommendations
- Automated design generation
- Optimal release timing for trending content

### Projected Impact (from strategy docs)
- Year 1: $3-5M additional revenue from trend capitalization
- Year 2: $8-12M from full AI system deployment
- Year 3: $15-25M from expanded personalization
